Here is the timeline of Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

  • 1931: Born on 15th October 1931 to a Tamilnadu Muslim family in Rameshwaram.
  • 1954: Graduated in Physics in 1954 from Madras University.
  • 1955: Moved to Madras in 1955 to study Aerospace Engineering in Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1960: Joined Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
  • 1969: Was transfered to Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  • 1980: Succesfully deployed the Rohini Satellite in near-earth orbit.
  • 1970-1999: Achivements- Project Devil, Project Valiant, Agni, Prithvi, Chief Scientific Advisor to the Defence Research and Development Orgabistaion (DRDO).
  • 2002-2007: He served as the President of INDIA.
  • 2015: He left the world.
